About the Firm

Four decades of trusted counsel

Founded in 1987, Van der Berg & Associates has built a reputation for delivering meticulous legal work in property transactions, commercial agreements, estate planning, and family matters.

We believe in thorough preparation, clear communication, and billing transparency. Our clients are entrepreneurs, families, and property investors who value senior-level attention without the overhead of a large firm.

Property Law
